Welcome To Sweet’nSalty Game!


How To Play The Game:


1) Enter your starting number. Then click Enter
2) Enter your ending number. Then click Enter
3) For every number that is a multiple of 3 SWEET will show instead.
4) For every number that is a multiple of 5 SALTY will show instead.
5) For every number that is a multiple of both Sweet’nSalty will show instead.
6) Lastly we will add up the total of all the words shown and let you know how many of each you got !
